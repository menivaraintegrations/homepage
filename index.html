Common Questions

What platforms can you integrate?
+
We work with virtually any platform that has an API—from major platforms like Slack, Google Workspace, ClickUp, and Salesforce to niche industry tools. If it has an API or integration capability, we can likely connect it.
How long does it take to implement an automation?
+
Timeline varies based on complexity. Simple automations can be deployed in a few days, while comprehensive multi-system integrations may take several weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.
What happens if something breaks?
+
All our automations include monitoring and error handling. If an issue occurs, we're alerted immediately and work to resolve it quickly. Our support packages ensure you're never left dealing with broken workflows alone.
Do I need technical knowledge to use these automations?
+
No technical knowledge is required. We design automations to run seamlessly in the background. We also provide documentation and training so your team understands what's happening and can make simple adjustments if needed.
Can automations be modified as my business changes?
+
Absolutely. We build flexible solutions that can evolve with your business. Whether you add new tools, change workflows, or scale operations, we can adjust your automations accordingly.
